# FUTURE_DS_03
This project analyzes the banking marketing funnel and customer conversion process using Power BI to track leads, conversions, retention, and customer behavior across different stages of the banking journey.
The purpose of this analysis is to help banks understand customer behavior, improve marketing strategies, increase conversion rates, and enhance customer retention.

Marketing Funnel Stages in Banking
	1.	Awareness
Potential customers become aware of the bank through marketing campaigns, advertisements, or referrals.
	2.	Interest
Customers show interest by visiting the bank website, downloading the mobile app, or requesting information about banking products.
	3.	Consideration
Customers compare banking services such as savings accounts, loans, or credit cards.
	4.	Conversion
Customers open an account or sign up for a banking product.
	5.	Retention
Customers continue using the bank’s services over time.

Key Metrics in the Funnel

Your Power BI dashboard can track:
	•	Total Leads
	•	Qualified Leads
	•	New Accounts Opened
	•	Conversion Rate
	•	Customer Retention Rate
	•	Churn Rate
	•	Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Example Calculations

Conversion Rate

Conversion Rate =
(Number of Customers Converted ÷ Total Leads) × 100

Example:
200 conversions ÷ 1,000 leads × 100 = 20% Conversion Rate

Churn Rate

Churn Rate =
(Customers Lost ÷ Total Customers) × 100

Example:
50 lost customers ÷ 500 total customers × 100 = 10% Churn Rat
